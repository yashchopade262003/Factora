Problem Statement

Title: Unorganized Factory Management System

Many small and medium-scale factories still rely on manual methods such as paper records, spreadsheets, and verbal communication to manage raw materials, production processes, packaging, dispatch, and buyer information. This lack of a centralized system leads to inefficient tracking of materials, poor coordination between departments, production delays, inventory mismatches, security issues, and difficulty in monitoring the status of products throughout the manufacturing lifecycle.

The absence of real-time visibility and automated workflow management makes it challenging for factory owners and supervisors to make informed decisions, optimize resources, and ensure timely product delivery to buyers.

Therefore, there is a need for an integrated Unorganized Factory Management System that can digitally manage and monitor the complete workflow—from raw material procurement and processing to packaging, dispatch, and buyer delivery—while providing real-time tracking, inventory management, role-based access control, and automated alerts to improve operational efficiency, transparency, and productivity.
